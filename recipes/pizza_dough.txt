Flour (40% spelt, 40% wheat, 20% buckwheat)
Yeast (fresh one is the best, not more than 50 degree)
Water
Salt
Sugar 
oil

1. Mix flour
2. make a hole in the flour
3. Mix warm water, yeast and sugar in the hole
4. Let it rest for 30 min
5. Add salt and oil
6. Mix it all
7. Let it rest for another 30 min
